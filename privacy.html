Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Kirtania ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of users ("you" or "your") who use our mobile applications. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Android applications available on Google Play.

By using our applications, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use our applications.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Information You Provide: Data you voluntarily enter into our applications, such as notes, text, or settings preferences.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our applications, including features used, session duration, and in-app actions.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and general diagnostic data.
  • Crash and Performance Data: Technical data related to application crashes, errors, and performance metrics used to improve stability.

3. How Information Is Used

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our applications and services.
  • To personalize your experience within our applications.
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize application performance.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, bugs, and crashes.
  • To communicate important updates or changes related to our applications.

4. Firebase Services

Our applications may use Firebase services provided by Google. These services may include:

Authentication

We may use Firebase Authentication to allow users to sign in securely. Authentication data, such as email addresses or unique user identifiers, is processed by Firebase in accordance with Google's privacy policies.

Analytics

We may use Firebase Analytics (Google Analytics for Firebase) to collect anonymized usage data. This helps us understand how users interact with our applications and identify areas for improvement. Analytics data is aggregated and does not personally identify individual users.

Crash Reporting

We may use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic data. This information is used solely to identify, diagnose, and resolve application crashes and stability issues.

For more information about how Firebase handles data, please review Firebase Privacy and Security documentation.

5. Permissions

Our applications may request certain device permissions to function properly. These permissions are used solely for their intended purposes:

  • Storage: To save and retrieve user-created content such as notes or files.
  • Internet: To sync data, check for updates, and communicate with backend services.
  • Notifications: To send reminders or important alerts to users.

We only request permissions that are necessary for the core functionality of each application. You can manage app permissions at any time through your device settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide the services described in this policy or as required by applicable law. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

User-created content stored locally on your device remains under your control and can be deleted at any time by clearing the application data or uninstalling the application.

Our applications may contain links to or integrate with third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access through our applications.

Third-party services that may be used include:

We implement industry-standard security practices, including encrypted data transmission and secure data storage, to protect your information.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email address below, and we will take steps to delete such information promptly.
